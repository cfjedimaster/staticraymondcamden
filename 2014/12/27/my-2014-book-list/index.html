My 2014 Book List

2014-12-27
Books

Presented just for the heck of it, here is the list of books I finished in 2014. Like last year, I’m a bit disappointed, but considering I doubled the amount of kids in my house, I think I have a valid excuse. My favorite of the year? Probably “Spin.” But I enjoyed the hell out of the Merchant Princes series. (My favorite genre is alt-history and I especially enjoy alt-history books that mix in our world as well.) My least favorite was “The Uncertain Places.” I nearly gave up about halfway through.
